## Where to Find All Features - Complete Navigation Guide

### Main Dashboard (Left Sidebar)

#### Story Phases (Main Journey)
- **Overview** - Home dashboard with quick stats, active clients, overdue tasks, activity feed
- **My Tasks Today** - Personal task list with due dates and priorities
- **Story Research** - Research phase with checklist and phase completion flow
- **Story Writing** - Writing phase with content management
- **Story Design & Video** - Design phase with creative assets
- **Story Website** - Website phase with strategy
- **Story Distribution** - Distribution phase with channel management
- **Story Analytics** - Analytics phase with real-time metrics and reporting
- **Story Learning** - Learning phase with retrospectives and improvements
- **Client Report Card** - Client-specific performance dashboard

#### Collaboration Section
- **Team Collaboration** - Activity feed, team mentions, and collaboration hub
- **Team Meetings** ⭐ NEW - Weekly team meeting scheduler, MOM tracker, and meeting history per client

#### Settings & Tools Section
- **Manage Campaigns** - Campaign creation and management
- **Record Metrics** - Metrics recording interface
- **Victory Targets** - Department and client victory targets
- **Content Calendar** ⭐ NEW - Visual content calendar with blackout dates, publishing frequency, platform-specific schedules
- **Compliance & Safety** ⭐ NEW - Approval chains, compliance checklist, brand safety filters, audit trail
- **Templates & SOPs** ⭐ NEW - Reusable content templates, process playbooks, SOP documentation
- **Manage Clients** - View, edit, add clients
- **Social Connections** - Connect social media platforms

---

### Within Client Detail View (Click on Client Card)

When you click on a client from the Overview dashboard, you see a tabbed interface:

1. **Project Overview** - Phase timeline, KPIs, team members, project metrics
2. **Team Meetings** - Weekly meeting scheduler, notes, attendees, action items for this specific client
3. **Tasks & Reporting** - All tasks for client with timeline/week/month filters, export capability
4. **Weekly Report** - Generate and send weekly reports to stakeholders

### New Features Overview

#### Content Calendar with Blackout Dates
- Location: `Settings → Content Calendar` (Sidebar)
- Shows: Month view of all scheduled posts by platform
- Features:
  - Publishing frequency analysis per platform
  - Blackout date management (holidays, maintenance, events)
  - Optimal posting times recommendations
  - Color-coded by platform with status indicators
  - Frequency warning system

#### Compliance & Brand Safety  
- Location: `Settings → Compliance & Safety` (Sidebar)
- Features:
  - Custom approval chain builder
  - 6+ pre-configured compliance rules
  - Pre-publication safety checklist (8 items)
  - Complete audit trail with timestamps
  - Severity levels (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
  - Rule violation tracking and reporting

#### Template Library & SOPs
- Location: `Settings → Templates & SOPs` (Sidebar)
- Includes:
  - 6 reusable content templates (LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, Email, Blog)
  - Template marketplace with search and filtering
  - 4 process playbooks (Story Research, Content Approval phases)
  - SOP documentation hub with 4 categories
  - Publish/draft status tracking
  - Rating and favorite system

#### Team Meeting Scheduler
- Location: `Collaboration → Team Meetings` (Sidebar) OR `Client Detail → Team Meetings Tab`
- Features:
  - Schedule weekly recurring meetings
  - Track attendance with attendee roles
  - Record meeting notes and action items
  - Meeting history with expandable details
  - Recording links support
  - Per-client weekly meetings

#### Client Tasks Overview with Filters
- Location: `Client Detail → Tasks & Reporting Tab`
- Filters available:
  - Timeline (This Week, Last Week, This Month, Last Month, All Time)
  - Status (Pending, In Progress, Completed)
  - Phase (Story Research, Writing, Design, etc.)
- Actions:
  - Export tasks to report (TXT format)
  - View task details with assignees
  - Track completion rates

#### Weekly Report Generator
- Location: `Client Detail → Weekly Report Tab`
- Features:
  - Edit-in-place accomplishments, blockers, priorities
  - Add/remove items dynamically
  - Automatic task summary integration
  - Email recipients management
  - Export as downloadable file
  - Preview before sending

---

### Key Integration Points

1. **Dashboard Home** → Click client card → Opens Client Detail View
2. **Team Meetings** → Visible in both Sidebar and Client Detail View
3. **Tasks & Reports** → Accessible from Client Detail View with data-driven insights
4. **Compliance** → Global setting, applies to all content pre-publication
5. **Templates** → Used across all phases for consistency
6. **Content Calendar** → Shows all scheduled content across all clients
